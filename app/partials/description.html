Let's get started!


Welcome to SpotiQuiz. To continue with the quiz click the "Play" button on the bottom of the page. After clicking the button you will be directed to the page where you will have a question on the screen followed by four options to choose from. The music will automatically play for 10 seconds upon the start of the quiz and on change of the question. If you correctly answer the question you will be awarded one point. There will be a time bar on the top of the screen indicating the time left for you to answer the question. Another bar on the bottom of the screen will indicate the progress in the quiz.

Power Ups!

You will have the option of using two "power ups" during the course of the game. The two power ups are named "Extra Time" and "50/50". The extra time power up will aloww you to fill up your time bar to a maximum so that you get more time to answer the question in in case you think that you know the answer but need more time to think. The "50/50" power up will randomly remove two incorrect options and leave you with one correct and one incorrect option to choose from. Both the power ups can be used only once during the course of the game.
